# Where to Find "Publish Directory" in Netlify UI

## Step-by-Step Instructions

### Method 1: Via Site Settings (Recommended)

1. **Go to your site dashboard:**
   - Visit: https://app.netlify.com/sites/neo-eligibility
   - Or go to: https://app.netlify.com and click on "neo-eligibility"

2. **Navigate to Build Settings:**
   - Click on **"Site configuration"** in the left sidebar (gear icon)
   - OR click on **"Build & deploy"** in the left sidebar
   - Then click on **"Build settings"** tab

3. **Find Publish Directory:**
   - Scroll down to the **"Build settings"** section
   - Look for a field labeled **"Publish directory"** or **"Base directory"**
   - It might be under "Build & deploy settings" or "Build configuration"

### Method 2: Via Deploy Settings

1. Go to: https://app.netlify.com/sites/neo-eligibility/configuration/deploys
2. Scroll to **"Build settings"** section
3. Look for **"Publish directory"**

### Method 3: Direct URL

Try this direct link:
**https://app.netlify.com/sites/neo-eligibility/configuration/deploys#build-settings**

### What to Look For

The field might be labeled as:
- "Publish directory"
- "Publish directory (optional)"
- "Base directory"
- "Output directory"
